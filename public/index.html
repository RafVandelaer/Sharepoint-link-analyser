Why do I need to run the script myself?

Privacy & Security: By running the script yourself in your own environment, we have no access to your SharePoint data. This is the safest way to ensure compliance and privacy.

Performance: SharePoint scans can take a long time for large tenants. By running locally, you can execute the scan at your own pace without server limitations.

Control: You have complete control over which sites are scanned, what data is collected, and where the results are stored.
